Recommendations

There are some key factors we should be adressing:
-Satisfaction:
Conduct Regular Surveys Implement regular surveys, use the survey to identify dissatisfaction trends or issues. We could keep the surveys anonymous to encourage honest feedback
Career Development Offer training programs, mentoring, and career growth path for those want to achive professional goals
-Salary:
Performance based compensation Implement performance bonus such as commissions, stock options, or cash to employee exceed expectations
-Filled Complaints:
Address Complaints Promptly this is the most effective approach. We should make sure that employees feel heared and there are corporate action to resolve those
Analyze the Nature of Complaints Categorize complaints to identify common themes, such as team conflicts, workload concerns, harassment, and other recurring challenges, to better understand the root causes.
