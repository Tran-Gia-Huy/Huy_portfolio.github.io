TIP & GO

My TipCo project report is available here.

Overview

Right off the bat, you might wonder why the project is named Tip & Go, not TipCo, like the company name. This project aims to introduce a new approach to reach customers with convenience and speed through TipCo’s vending machine strategy.

Current Challenges and Solution

Current Challenges

  • 1. Long cash conversion cycle
  • 2. High cost per sale
  • 3. Increased competition

Proposed Solution

The Tip & Go vending machines:
Innovative way to reduce costs, improve speed, meet the growing demand for fresh and healthy options.

Market Opportunity

The market for vending machines and fresh juices is growing rapidly. This strategy fits well within this growth trend, leveraging TipCo’s strengths.

Corporate and Business Strategy

This strategy aligns with TipCo's horizontal growth initiatives. The vending machines will be placed in high-traffic locations, focusing on customer convenience and differentiation.

Marketing Strategy

Our campaign includes partnerships with brand ambassadors, customer engagement on social media, and promotions to establish brand presence and customer loyalty.

Product Menu and Pricing

The vending machines will offer a variety of juices and juice sodas, with competitive pricing aimed at customer affordability and brand accessibility.

Financial Projections

Expected revenue growth and enhanced intrinsic value over the next three years are projected to solidify TipCo’s market presence.

Scenario and Simulation Analysis

The analysis includes DCF and Monte Carlo simulations, assessing best- and worst-case revenue scenarios.

Implementation Plan

This section details our phased approach to deploying vending machines and advertising the Tip & Go brand.

Risk Assessment and Contingency Planning

Identified risks include changes in customer behavior and raw material costs. Mitigation strategies are in place to manage these risks effectively.
